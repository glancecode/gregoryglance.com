What We Offer

Our Services

From short-term rehabilitation to long-term skilled nursing, we provide a full continuum of care tailored to each resident's needs.

Skilled Nursing

Round-the-clock nursing care for residents requiring ongoing medical attention. Our licensed nurses provide compassionate, professional care in a comfortable environment.

Learn more

Short-Term Rehabilitation

Post-hospital recovery programs featuring physical, occupational, and speech therapy. Our goal is to help you regain independence and return home stronger.

Learn more

Memory Care

Specialized programs for residents living with Alzheimer's disease and other forms of dementia. Our trained staff create safe, structured, and engaging daily routines.

Learn more

Respite Care

Temporary stays that give family caregivers a well-deserved break. Your loved one receives full access to our amenities and clinical services during their visit.

Learn more

Long-Term Care

Permanent skilled nursing for residents who need continuous medical supervision. We create a home-like atmosphere with personalized care plans and enriching activities.

Why Progressive Quality Care

Built on a Foundation of Family Values

For more than a decade, Progressive Quality Care has been providing high-quality skilled nursing, rehabilitation, and memory care services. As a family-owned organization, we understand the importance of treating every resident like a member of our own family.

  • Family-Owned & Operated

    Our leadership team brings personal commitment and accountability to every facility, ensuring consistent quality across our entire network.

  • Personalized Care Plans

    Every resident receives an individualized care plan developed with input from their family, physicians, and our interdisciplinary clinical team.

  • Hotel-Like Amenities

    Our facilities feature all-private suites, restaurant-style dining, scenic campuses, and life enrichment programs that make residents feel at home.
