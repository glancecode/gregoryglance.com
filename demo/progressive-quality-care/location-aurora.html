About Our Facility

Avenue at Aurora is a 98-bed skilled nursing and rehabilitation facility located in Aurora, Ohio. Conveniently situated close to local hospitals and medical centers, our facility provides a distinctive hotel-like environment designed to support healing and comfort.

Residents enjoy all-private suites, restaurant-style dining, and a scenic campus that promotes wellness and relaxation. Our interdisciplinary clinical team works closely with each resident and their family to develop personalized care plans that address individual health goals and preferences.

Our Services

Avenue at Aurora offers a comprehensive range of care services, including skilled nursing, short-term rehabilitation, memory care, respite stays, and long-term care. Our rehabilitation program features physical, occupational, and speech therapy designed to help residents recover and regain independence after surgery, illness, or injury.

Daily Life

We believe that quality of life extends beyond clinical care. At Avenue at Aurora, residents enjoy flexible daily schedules tailored to their personal preferences. Meals are served throughout the day in our restaurant-style dining room, offering chef-prepared menus with attention to both nutrition and taste.

Our life enrichment programs include social events, creative activities, fitness programs, and community outings. We encourage family involvement and welcome visitors to participate in the daily life of our community.
